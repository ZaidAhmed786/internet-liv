FAQs

Get the Answers to Common Questions

We offer a range of internet plans designed to suit different needs, from basic browsing to high-speed streaming and gaming. To choose the right plan, consider the number of devices you’ll connect, your online activities, and how many people will be using the internet simultaneously. Our customer support team can also assist you in selecting a plan that matches your usage and budget.

We utilize advanced network management techniques to optimize traffic flow and prevent congestion, especially during peak usage hours. Our infrastructure is built to handle high demand, ensuring you receive consistent speeds, even when multiple users are online. We also monitor our network performance regularly to quickly address any issues that may affect your experience.

If your internet connection is unstable, start by rebooting your router and modem. Ensure all cables are securely connected and not damaged. You can also check if any software updates are needed for your router. If the issue persists, contact our technical support team, who can assist with troubleshooting or schedule a technician visit if necessary.

Installation typically takes between 1 to 3 business days, depending on your location and our availability. Once you schedule an appointment, a technician will visit your home to set up the necessary equipment and test your connection to ensure everything is working properly. We’ll keep you updated throughout the process and work to install your service as quickly as possible.
